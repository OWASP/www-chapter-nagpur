## Call for Speakers

We are always looking for passionate speakers to present at our OWASP Nagpur Chapter meetups!

Whether you are a security researcher, developer, penetration tester, student, or enthusiast — if you have knowledge to share, we want to hear from you. All talks must be **vendor-neutral** and aligned with OWASP's open and non-commercial values.

### Topics We Love

- OWASP Top 10 (Web, API, Mobile)
- Web & Mobile Application Security
- Cloud Security & DevSecOps
- Penetration Testing & Ethical Hacking
- Threat Modeling & Secure Design
- Capture The Flag (CTF) walkthroughs
- Privacy, Compliance & GDPR
- AI/ML Security
- Any independent security research

### How to Submit

📧 Send your speaker proposal to **[aishwary.gathe@owasp.org](mailto:aishwary.gathe@owasp.org)** with the subject line: `[Speaker Proposal] <Your Talk Title>`

Please include the following details in your email:

**Speaker Information**
- Full Name
- Job Role / Title
- Company / Organization
- City & Country
- Email ID
- Contact Number
- LinkedIn / GitHub / Speaker Profile (optional)

**Talk Details**
- Title of the Presentation
- Abstract / Summary (200–400 words)
- Estimated Presentation Duration (20 / 30 / 45 minutes)
- Talk Level (Beginner / Intermediate / Advanced)
- Any prerequisites for the audience

### What We Offer

- A welcoming and engaged audience of security professionals & enthusiasts
- Promotion of your talk on our [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/company/owaspngp/) page
- A great networking opportunity with the Nagpur security community
- Recording / slides shared with our community (with your permission)

### Speaker Agreement

By presenting at an OWASP Nagpur Chapter event, speakers agree to the [OWASP Speaker Agreement](https://owasp.org/www-policy/operational/speaker-agreement). All presentations must be vendor-neutral and cannot be used to sell products or services.

---

> 💡 **Note:** You don't need to be an expert to speak! Lightning talks (5–10 minutes), demos, and beginner-level sessions are very welcome. We value diverse perspectives from all backgrounds.
